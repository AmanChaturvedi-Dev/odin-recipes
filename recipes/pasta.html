Pasta

Description

Pasta is a traditional Italian dish made from wheat flour and water, often with eggs, and formed into various shapes. It is typically boiled and served with a wide variety of sauces, such as tomato-based, cream-based, or oil-based sauces.

Pasta is loved for its versatility and can be paired with vegetables, meats, cheeses, and herbs to create endless delicious combinations.

Ingredients

  • 200g dried pasta (spaghetti, penne, fusilli, etc.)
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped) or 1 cup tomato puree
  • Salt to taste
  • ½ teaspoon black pepper
  • ½ teaspoon chili flakes (optional)
  • Mixed herbs or Italian seasoning
  • Grated cheese (optional, for garnish)
  • Fresh basil or parsley (optional, for garnish)

Steps

  1. Boil water in a large pot and add a pinch of salt.
  2. Add the pasta and cook according to the package instructions until al dente.
  3. Drain the pasta and set it aside.
  4. Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  5. Add minced garlic and chopped onion. Sauté until translucent.
  6. Add chopped tomatoes or tomato puree. Cook until the sauce thickens.
  7. Season with salt, black pepper, chili flakes, and mixed herbs.
  8. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss well to combine.
  9. Let it cook for another 1–2 minutes to absorb the flavors.
  10. Garnish with grated cheese and fresh basil or parsley before serving.

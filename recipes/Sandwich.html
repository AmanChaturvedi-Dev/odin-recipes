Grilled Cheese Sandwich

Description

The classic grilled cheese sandwich is gooey, crispy, and satisfyingâ€”perfect for a quick meal or snack.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese (cheddar or your choice)
  • 1 tablespoon butter

Steps

  1. Heat a pan on medium heat.
  2. Butter one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice, butter side down, on the pan. Add cheese on top.
  4. Top with the second slice of bread, butter side up.
  5. Grill until golden brown, then flip and cook the other side until cheese is melted and bread is crisp.
  6. Remove from pan, slice, and serve hot.
